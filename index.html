System Architecture

How GeoRisk AI works

Four layered systems transform raw open-source intelligence into calibrated collapse probability estimates.

▸ Data Ingestion
Multimodal Intelligence Collection
Continuous pipelines ingest satellite imagery, economic indicators, conflict event databases, refugee flow statistics, and social media signals — unified into a single temporal feature store.
NASA EarthdataACLEDWorld BankUNHCRGDELT
▸ Fusion Engine
Multimodal Feature Fusion
CNN encoders process satellite imagery. LSTM networks capture temporal economic signals. Graph Neural Networks model inter-state dependency. A transformer cross-attention layer fuses all modalities.
CNNLSTMGNNTransformerPyTorch
▸ Causal Inference
Why States Fail — Not Just When
Structural Causal Models identify intervention points. DoWhy + CausalML separate confounders from true drivers. This answers "what caused this trajectory" — enabling targeted policy response.
DoWhyCausalMLSCMCounterfactual
▸ Forecasting
Long-Horizon Risk Scoring
XGBoost ensemble + Bayesian uncertainty quantification generates risk scores at 6, 12, 24, 36, and 60-month horizons with calibrated confidence intervals — critical for policy planning.
XGBoostBayesian UQConformal Prediction
Feature Engineering

8 composite risk indicators

Each indicator is computed from multiple raw data sources, normalized, and fed into the fusion model as a weighted composite score.

Economic Instability
GDP growth anomaly, inflation rate, currency volatility, external debt ratio, foreign reserve depletion
World Bank · IMF · FRED
Political Fragility
Government effectiveness, coup attempt frequency, election integrity scores, opposition suppression index
Freedom House · V-Dem · Polity5
Social Unrest
Protest frequency and size, media freedom index, income inequality (Gini), ethnic tension indicators
ACLED · RSF · World Inequality DB
Food Insecurity
NDVI satellite anomaly, food price index deviation, import dependency ratio, stunting prevalence
FAO · NASA MODIS · WFP
Military Tension
Arms import volume, border incident frequency, paramilitary group activity, military spending ratio
SIPRI · ACLED · Jane's
Migration Pressure
Displacement rate, asylum application volume, internal migration anomaly, brain drain rate
UNHCR · IOM · World Bank
Infrastructure Collapse
Power outage frequency (nighttime lights satellite), road connectivity index, hospital density, sanitation access
NOAA VIIRS · OSM · WHO
Climate Vulnerability
Drought frequency index, flood risk score, temperature anomaly from baseline, crop yield deviation
ERA5 · NASA GRACE · EM-DAT
Research Roadmap

Where we are. Where we're going.

A transparent view of the research timeline — from prototype to publication to government-grade system.

Phase 1 — Complete
Prototype Dashboard & Indicator Framework Done
8-indicator scoring system, interactive visualization, AI-powered intelligence briefs via Claude API, historical trajectory modeling.
Phase 2 — In Progress
Real Data Pipeline Integration Active
Connecting NASA Earthdata, World Bank API, ACLED, and UNHCR feeds. Building normalized feature store with automated refresh cycles.
Phase 3 — Q3 2025
Multimodal ML Model Training Upcoming
CNN + LSTM + Transformer fusion model trained on historical collapse events (1995–2024). Backtesting framework on 47 labeled events.
Phase 4 — Q4 2025
Causal Inference Module Upcoming
DoWhy integration for structural causal modeling. Counterfactual analysis: "What would have happened if food prices hadn't spiked?" Intervention point identification.
Phase 5 — Q1 2026
Research Publication Upcoming
Target: NeurIPS 2026 / AAAI 2026. "Multimodal Causal Fusion for Long-Horizon Geopolitical Instability Prediction." Open benchmark dataset release.
Phase 6 — 2026
Government & NGO Pilot Integration Upcoming
REST API deployment. Pilot partnerships with humanitarian organizations. Real-time alert system with diplomatic intervention workflow integration.
